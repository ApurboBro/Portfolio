Exploring Image Classification with Deep Learning

Image classification is a fundamental task in computer vision, involving the identification of objects, scenes, or concepts within images. Deep learning has revolutionized this domain, enabling systems to achieve remarkable accuracy and scalability.

Key Highlights

  • Deep Learning Models: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) dominate image classification, thanks to their ability to detect patterns like edges and textures.
  • Applications:
    • Healthcare: Detecting diseases in medical imaging.
    • Autonomous Vehicles: Real-time road sign recognition.
    • Retail: Automated product tagging.
  • Future Scope: Integration with AI to enhance real-time decision-making.
